<research_focus>
When researching company history, focus on:

1. **Founding Story**
   - When and where was the company founded?
   - Who are the founders? What were their backgrounds?
   - What was the original vision/mission?
   - What problem were they trying to solve?

2. **Product Evolution**
   - What were the early products/services?
   - What were the key product pivots or transitions?
   - What are the major product lines today?
   - Which products drive the most revenue?

3. **Key Milestones**
   - IPO date and initial valuation
   - Major acquisitions (name, price, rationale)
   - Geographic expansion or market entry milestones
   - Regulatory approvals or significant partnerships
   - Technology breakthroughs or patents

4. **Stock Performance Context**
   - Major stock price movements and their triggers
   - Stock splits or buyback programs
   - Dividend history (if applicable)
   - Market cap evolution (include current market cap)
</research_focus>
